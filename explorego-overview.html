What it does

It gives travel companies an interactive way to inspire travellers earlier in the journey, using curated destination content, filters, maps and discovery logic to guide users towards relevant places.

Why it matters

Static destination lists are often too passive. Explore & Go helps travel brands create a more dynamic discovery journey that can increase engagement and move travellers closer to booking.

Destination discovery

Help travellers explore relevant destinations by interest, context, market or travel motivation.

Interactive experience

Use maps, filters, destination cards and visual journeys rather than static destination pages.

Inspiration to conversion

Move users from browsing into more concrete trip consideration.

Content-rich journeys

Combine destination descriptions, imagery, events and points of interest.

White-label flexibility

Adapt the experience to the brand, product and customer journey.

Campaign-ready

Useful for airline route promotion, OTA inspiration, seasonal campaigns and destination marketing.

Business value

  • More engagement at the inspiration stage.
  • Better visibility of destinations, routes and campaigns.
  • A more modern discovery experience for travellers.
  • Clearer bridge between inspiration and booking.
