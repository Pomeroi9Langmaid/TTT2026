What it does

It connects destination content, maps, POIs, events and planning functionality so travellers can move from inspiration into practical trip planning before and during travel.

Why it matters

Travel companies need to keep travellers engaged beyond the booking. Trip Planner creates a useful planning layer that adds value, improves destination engagement and strengthens the digital experience.

Itinerary planning

Let travellers save, organise and structure places they want to visit.

Destination content

Combine POIs, restaurants, events and practical information in one experience.

Interactive maps

Make planning more visual and intuitive through map-led exploration.

Before and during travel

Support engagement before departure and once travellers arrive.

Brand-controlled UX

White-label deployment aligned to the client’s visual identity.

API and widget options

Use the planning layer inside existing web or app journeys.

Business value

  • Stronger destination engagement after booking.
  • A more useful digital experience for travellers.
  • More opportunities to surface experiences, events and local content.
  • Better traveller support across the planning journey.
