Use case

Where this matters

For airlines, airports, cruise operators and travel platforms supporting travellers with reduced mobility, disabilities, mobility equipment or specific assistance needs.

Before booking • Assistance request • Departure airport • Boarding • In-flight • Arrival airport
The problem

Accessibility information exists — but it is fragmented

Travellers are not only asking whether a journey is available. They need to know whether the journey will actually work for them.

Unclear expectations What happens at boarding? Where is assistance confirmed? What should the traveller expect?
Route-specific uncertainty Airport procedures, airline rules, equipment handling and assistance points vary by route and context.
Operational pressure When information is unclear, uncertainty moves into support teams, airport operations and ground handling.
