TravelAble
Accessible travel support

Accessibility travel information, structured around the real journey.

TravelAble turns fragmented accessibility information into clear, personalised guidance — helping travellers understand what will happen before, during and after their trip.

Use case

Where this matters

For airlines, airports, cruise operators and travel platforms supporting travellers with reduced mobility, disabilities, mobility equipment or specific assistance needs.

Clear benefits for travel teams

Useful for airlines, airports and support teams.

TravelAble gives teams a clearer way to communicate accessibility information across the digital journey.

Less friction in assistance and SSR selection Fewer repetitive queries and reduced pre-trip anxiety Better alignment with handling and operational processes Consistent, inclusive communication
Governance, technology and compliance

Official structured PRM information.

TravelAble is based on official airline and airport sources and is continuously monitored to keep information up to date.

Structured content, not scattered policy text Designed to align with WCAG 2.0 AA Supports consistent accessibility communication
Validated with those who need it most

Built with real travellers.

TravelAble was shaped through interviews with wheelchair users and accessibility associations, identifying real friction points throughout the journey.

Uncertainty during security and boarding Risks in equipment handling Communication gaps between teams and travellers
