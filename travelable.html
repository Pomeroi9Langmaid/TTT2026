TravelAble
Accessible travel support

Make assisted travel clearer.

TravelAble helps airlines communicate accessibility and PRM information more clearly across the traveller journey — helping passengers understand what support is available, what they need to prepare, and how the process works before they travel.

Journey-specific guidanceInformation can be personalised by route, airline, airport, aircraft and traveller needs.
Wheelchair + battery rulesSupport for wheelchair type, battery details, mobility equipment and related travel conditions.
Airport + airline detailsWhat to expect before the flight, at the airport, onboard and on arrival.
Reduced uncertaintyGive travellers clearer self-service answers before they contact customer support.
Clear benefits for travel teams

Useful for airline accessibility and support teams.

TravelAble gives teams a clearer way to communicate accessibility information across the digital journey.

Less friction in assistance and SSR selection Fewer repetitive queries and reduced pre-trip anxiety Better alignment with handling and operational processes Consistent, inclusive communication
Governance, technology and compliance

Official structured PRM information.

TravelAble is based on official airline and airport sources and is continuously monitored to keep information up to date.

Structured content, not scattered policy text Designed to align with WCAG 2.0 AA Supports consistent accessibility communication
Validated with those who need it most

Built with real travellers.

TravelAble was shaped through interviews with wheelchair users and accessibility associations, identifying real friction points throughout the journey.

Uncertainty during security and boarding Risks in equipment handling Communication gaps between teams and travellers
Future of TravelAble

From PRM to broader accessibility coverage.

TravelAble starts with PRM and evolves toward wider coverage across visual, auditory, cognitive and hidden disabilities.

Medical equipment filters, including oxygen devices More personalised journey guidance Deeper connection support across travel stages
